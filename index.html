Welcome to the Tap Speed Study

How it works:

You'll complete two rounds of 50 taps each (100 taps total):

  • Round 1: Press and hold the button, then release it - do this 50 times. You may or may not see your average hold time.
  • Round 2: Repeat the same process for another 50 taps. The feedback display will switch (if you saw it before, you won't now, and vice versa)

This helps us study whether seeing your performance affects your tapping speed!
